Know your customer path to sale & Becoming a student of your customer

Knowing your customers is not enough. You also need a thorough understanding of how their values, behavioral characteristics, traits, and personality traits translate into sales with your company. You have to understand customer’s organization, new developments, culture, industry, value proposition, their customer, partners, competitors, people…

  • What are the most common ways that prospects find out about your company?
  • What are some of the initial questions they’re asking?
  • What is the typical decision-making process for buying? What kinds of follow-up questions are they asking, and what types of stakeholders are involved with conversations about your brand?
  • How long does the overall process last? About how much time does each stage take?
  • What’s going on in your customer’s business?
  • How is your customer perceived within their industry?
  • Do you understand your customer’s value proposition(s)?
  • Have you identitified key people within your customer’s organization?
